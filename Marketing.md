## Positioning & Taglines

**Core position**: The narrowest, fastest change-order approval tool for small field service contractors — not another CRM, not another all-in-one FSM platform.

**Taglines to test:**
- "Scope changed. Margin should not." *(current hero — test engagement)*
- "Capture the extra work before it eats the job."
- "Three panels. One clear yes." *(approval flow angle)*
- "Document it, price it, get a yes. Before the crew keeps moving."
- "Your client approved it. Now you can prove it."

**Positioning statement:**
For plumbers, AV installers, and small field service teams who lose margin on unapproved extra work, Change Order Tracker is the purpose-built approval workflow that captures scope changes onsite, sends a clean client decision link, and produces invoice-ready documentation — without the overhead of Jobber, Housecall Pro, or Buildertrend.

## Target Audiences

**Primary:** Owner-operators and office managers at plumbing shops (2-10 techs) in the US and Canada
**Secondary:** Commercial AV integrators and installation contractors handling variable-scope projects
**Tertiary:** Any small field service trade (electrical, HVAC, general) with recurring scope drift problems

## Competitor Gaps

### Jobber
- **Their strength**: Full FSM — scheduling, dispatch, invoicing, CRM
- **Their gap**: Not purpose-built for the "scope just changed on site, I need approval before I keep going" moment
- **Our pitch**: "Jobber is great if you need to run your whole business from one app. We are the tool for the one specific thing Jobber doesn't make fast enough: getting client approval before extra work happens."

### Housecall Pro
- **Their strength**: Field service operations, customer management, payments
- **Their gap**: Generic workflow; no first-class change-order documentation or approval experience
- **Our pitch**: "Housecall Pro handles the job. We handle the moment when the job changes."

### Buildertrend
- **Their strength**: Construction project management with change order support
- **Their gap**: Built for builders and GCs, too heavy for plumbing or AV service shops
- **Our pitch**: "Buildertrend is built for construction PMs. We are built for the plumber in a crawl space who needs the homeowner to approve two hours of extra labor before they continue."

## Viral Hooks

- **Free change order template** — A downloadable PDF template with the same fields as the app. High SEO value, easy shareability in plumbing forums. Convert to trial via "want this automated instead?"
- **"One job saved" story format** — "This plumber recovered $1,400 from a single disputed invoice because they had a timestamped approval." Shareable story format for social.
- **Live workflow demo** (/studio) — No login required, shows the full flow. Link drops work in forums without feeling like a sales pitch.
- **Approval link demo** (/approve/demo) — Send this URL to show prospects exactly what their clients would see. Useful in sales conversations.

## Timing Opportunities

- **Post-summer plumbing season (Sep-Oct)**: Many shops reassess tools after a busy season when billing disputes accumulate. Target this window for community outreach.
- **New year planning (Jan)**: Small business software buying cycle. Comparison content and "what are you using for change orders?" threads perform well.
- **CEDIA Expo** (annual, ~September): AV/CI industry event. Good timing for AV-specific push.
